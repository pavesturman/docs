# Billing & Subscriptions

## Plans

Advato offers flexible pricing with a 14-day free trial.

### Features included in all plans:

- Referral system ready out of the box
- Unlimited referrals
- Unlimited auto-prompting for referrals
- Unlimited app users
- Unlimited custom popup notifications
- Customizable UI components
- Deep link handling and attribution
- Remote configuration
- Analytics and tracking
- Secure authentication

## Managing Your Subscription

- Access billing portal through Dashboard > Settings > Billing
- Update payment method
- View invoices
- Change subscription plan
